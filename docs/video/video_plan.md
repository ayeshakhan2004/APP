# CIRO Video & Documentation Plan

## 🎥 Demo Video (3-5 mins)
1. **The Ingest**: Show raw signals (JSON) from 5 sources appearing on the screen.
2. **The Fusion**: Show the AI logs (Traces) detecting that "Flood" signals and "Pipe Burst" signals are conflicting.
3. **The Logic**: Show Gemini reasoning: "Choosing 'Pipe Burst' because IoT sensor is more credible than social media."
4. **The UI**: Show the Flutter Map with the incident marker.
5. **The Response**: Show the 'Resource Allocation' table sending a repair team instead of a boat.
6. **The Stakeholders**: Show the generated SMS for the public: "Water main repair in progress, avoid G-10."

## 🎥 Antigravity Usage Video (2-3 mins)
1. Screen recording of Antigravity building the 7 agents in 60 seconds.
2. Showing how Antigravity helped Sohail fix the "Quota Exceeded" or dependency issues.
3. Final view of the 35+ files generated by the AI.
